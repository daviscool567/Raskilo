  • What is BarPay

    BarPay is In-Bar order and pay app, which eliminates the hitch in finding a waiter and placing orders. you can also call it a Bar and restaurant Menu Manager. Bar Owners, can sign in with the Bar Owner app to manage his bar and withdraw Sales revenue.

  • Users or customers

    Customers will download the BarPay App to be able to place orders and pay from their wallets with ease and will not have to search for waiters anymore. By scanning the QR Code on each table, a customer can now enter the Bar's menu to place orders.
